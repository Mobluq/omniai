# BD Select Roadmap And Metrics

## North Star

Authenticated completed monthly GMV.

This metric intentionally includes authentication and completion so the team optimizes for trust, liquidity, and usable revenue instead of raw listings.

## KPIs

### Marketplace

- Monthly active buyers.
- Monthly active sellers.
- Listings approved per week.
- Search-to-purchase conversion.
- Sell-through within 60 days.
- Listing-to-approval median time.

### Trust

- Authentication accuracy: target 98%+ sampled.
- Dispute rate: target below 1.5% of completed orders.
- Refund rate: target below 2.0% of GMV.
- Median dispute resolution time: target below 36 hours.
- Buyer NPS: target 60+ rolling 30-day.

### Financial

- Blended take rate.
- Contribution margin per order.
- CAC, LTV, payback.
- Burn multiple.
- Cash runway.
